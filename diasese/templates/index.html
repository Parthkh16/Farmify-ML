About This Model

This application uses a ResNet50 deep learning model to classify:

  • Plant diseases across various crops (cotton, wheat, rice, etc.)
  • Healthy vs. rotten fruits
  • Pest infestations

The model was trained on 70 different classes with over 10,000 images and achieved 99.44% validation accuracy.
